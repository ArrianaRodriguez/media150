Homework #4: Social Campaign - Photo Montage


The concept this montage was meant to explore was the environmental damage that is being caused by mankind. In my photo, there's a buck and a fawn walking together through a flame-filled forest and ultimately staring off into what looks to be the culprits of the damage. A large, developed city. This of course is meant to be an overexaggeration– but is also meant to dive deep into the intensity of how badly things like deforestation, poaching, and other cruel events like these seem to put on the animals' well-being. Every year, more and more of nature is taken away for the benefit of industries and more.
I used highly saturated colors to purposefully put strain on the eyes and uncomfortability to almost put you in the animals' perspectives. Overlay really seemed to help me blend other photos into the red-ish to yellow color palette. Additionally, a clipping mask was used to give the deer and fawn their red hues so they feel too out of place. All photos I used to create my montage were online, non-copyrighted pieces. I took one buck, one of the fawn, three wildfire photos, and one photo of a city.
