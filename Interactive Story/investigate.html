Russel braces himself and slowly push aside a shrub blocking the view.


His eye's light up at the sight of his coworker, Mac! "Mac?! Hello! What are you doing way out here?"
The little monkey was startled by the sudden burst of noise ans turned to Russel before letting out a sigh of relief.

"Russel...? Phew.. It's good to see you, friend. I was trying to bring these two packages across town, but I didn't really realize how heavy they'd be. Long story short, I figured a walk through the forest would serve as a good shortcut. It Didnt. So I've been wandering aimlessy for the past 10 minutes.."
Russel chuckled. "Hahah.. Look's like I'm not the only one that choose a different delivery route today. I can help you out with a package If you'd like. We can travel together!"
"Oh, yeah..! I'd appreciate that. Thank you." He smiled and gently handed the package he was holding to Russel. Now grabbing the one that was being held by his tail.


The two little mailmen travelled through the lush greens, chatting to eachother and laughing off their silly situation. It takes some time, but they see a glimpse of the neighborhood past the trees!
